About The Event

About The Event

 IEEE STEP is a dynamic program that aims at final year students who are soon to make their professional sector debut. It's developed to facilitate the transition of a student member to a young professional by introducing the STEM career aspirants to the opportunities and benefits of IEEE membership.

 IEEE Young Professionals programme is a vibrant platform which helps the debutants the opportunities to advance skills, professional networks and confidence. It is organised in conjunction with large scale IEEE events such as conferences, region events or cross section activities and seeks to enhance volunteer satisfaction by supporting the volunteers to conduct engaging events.

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

LEAD College of Management Palakkad

LEAD College of Management, a state -of -the -art, stand alone MBA institute, approved by AICTE and affiliated to University of Calicut was established in 2011. LEAD is an acronym derived from 'Leadership & Entrepreneurship Academy, Dhoni'. LEAD envisions in creating an academic environment where highest standards of academics and professionalism are met and besides the expectations of all stakeholders are consciously addressed.
