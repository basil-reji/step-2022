About The Event

 IEEE STEP (Student Transition and Elevation Program) is a dynamic program that targets final year students who are soon to make their debut in the professional sector. It is developed to facilitate the transition from a student member to a young professional by introducing STEM career aspirants to the opportunities and benefits of an IEEE membership.

 It aims to introduce them to IEEE Young Professionals programs and provide a vibrant platform to build a professional network with Young Professionals in the same area. It is organized in conjunction with large-scale IEEE events such as conferences, region events or cross-section activities and seeks to enhance volunteer satisfaction by providing support for local volunteers and conducting engaging events.

LEAD is an acronym derived from 'Leadership & Entrepreneurship Academy, Dhoni'.
